\[\newcommand{\Z}{\mathbb Z}\]

This is a reading list for \(p\)-adic cohomology and its connections to homotopy theory, intended to help people interested in getting into the field. It is not exhaustive, and is biased towards my own interests and more recent papers.

Legend:

  • ► survey / good place to start
  • ■ course notes
  • ★ major / foundational paper

Big picture

  • Scholze ICM address
  • Bhatt ICM address
  • Elmanto, Motives at $p$
  • Kaledin ICM address

THH and TC

  • Hesselholt-Nikolaus, Topological cyclic homology (Handbook article)
  • Krause-Nikolaus, Lectures on THH and cyclotomic spectra
  • Nikolaus-Scholze
  • Krause-Nikolaus, Bökstedt periodicity and quotients of DVRs

Prismatic cohomology (THH perspective)

  • Mathew, Some recent advances in topological Hochschild homology
  • Morrow, THH in arithmetic geometry (AWS 2019)
  • Arbeitsgemeinschaft notes
  • BMS2
  • Anschütz-le Bras, The $p$-completed cyclotomic trace in degree 2

Prismatic cohomology (algebraic perspective)

  • Scholze, Canonical $q$-deformations in arithmetic geometry
  • Bhatt lecture notes
  • Kedlaya, Notes on prismatic cohomology
  • Bhatt-Scholze, Prisms and prismatic cohomology
  • Bhatt-Lurie, Absolute prismatic cohomology
  • Bhatt-Mathew, Syntomic complexes and $p$-adic étale Tate twists
  • Holeman, Derived $\delta$-Rings and Relative Prismatic Cohomology

Even filtration / THH of ring spectra

  • Hahn-Wilson, Redshift and multiplication for truncated Brown-Peterson spectra
  • Hahn-Raksit-Wilson, A motivic filtration on the topological cyclic homology of commutative ring spectra
  • Devalapurkar, Topological Hochschild homology, truncated Brown-Peterson spectra, and a topological Sen operator
  • Pstrągowski, Perfect even modules and the even filtration
  • Devalapurkar-Hahn-Lawson-Senger-Wilson, Examples of disk algebras
  • Devalapurkar-Misterka, Generalized $n$-series and de Rham complexes

Calculations

  • Lee, Integral topological Hochschild homology of connective complex K-theory
  • Liu-Wang, Topological Cyclic Homology of Local Fields
  • Riggenbach, On The Algebraic $K$-Theory of Double Points
  • Riggenbach, $K$-Theory of Cuspidal Curves Over a Perfectoid Base And Formal Analogues
  • Riggenbach, $K$-Theory of Truncated Polynomials
  • Speirs, On the K-theory of truncated polynomial algebras, revisited
  • Speirs, On the K-theory of coordinate axes in affine space
  • Sulyma, Floor, ceiling, slopes, and $K$-theory

Cartier modules / TR

  • Antieau-Nikolaus, Cartier modules and cyclotomic spectra
  • McCandless, On curves in $\mathrm K$-theory and $\mathrm{TR}$
  • Mondal, Dieudonné theory via cohomology of classifying stacks
  • Dotto-Krause-Nikolaus-Patchkoria, Witt vectors with coefficients and characteristic polynomials over non-commutative rings
  • Mathew, On $K(1)$-local $\mathrm{TR}$

Genuine equivariance

  • Hu-Kriz-Somberg, On the equivariant motivic filtration of the topological Hochschild homology of polynomial algebras
  • Sulyma, A slice refinement of Bökstedt periodicity
  • Sulyma, \(\mathrm{RO}(\mathbb T)\)-graded \(\mathrm{TF}\) of perfectoid rings

de Rham cohomology

  • Li-Mondal, On endomorphisms of the de Rham cohomology functor
  • Mondal, $\mathbb G_a^{\mathrm{perf}}$-modules and de Rham cohomology
  • Raksit, Hochschild homology and the derived de Rham complex revisited
  • Ariotta(-Krause), Coherent cochain complexes and Beilinson $t$-structures
  • Mao, Revisiting derived crystalline cohomology

de Rham-Witt stuff

  • Bhatt-Lurie-Mathew, Revisiting the de Rham-Witt complex
  • Molokov, Prismatic cohomology and de Rham-Witt forms
  • Random thoughts on Witt vectors

Hodge to de Rham degeneration

  • Mathew, Kaledin's degeneration theorem and topological Hochschild homology
  • Antieau-Bhatt-Mathew, Counterexamples to HKR in characteristic $p$
  • Petrov, Non-decomposability of the de Rham complex and non-semisimplicity of the Sen operator
  • Devalapurkar, Lifting to truncated Brown-Peterson spectra and Hodge-de Rham degeneration in characteristic $p>0$

Fargues-Fontaine curve

  • 2016 Arbeitsgemeinschaft
  • Lurie Fargues-Fontaine seminar, especially Lecture 1 is inspiring
  • Lurie Fargues-Fontaine course
  • Anschütz course notes
  • Hesselholt, Topological cyclic homology and the Fargues-Fontaine curve

Formal groups

  • Moulinos-Robalo-Toën, A universal HKR theorem
  • Moulinos, The geometry of filtrations
  • Moulinos, Filtered formal groups, Cartier duality, and derived algebraic geometry
  • Mondal-Reinecke, Unipotent homotopy theory of schemes

K-theory

  • Clausen-Mathew-Morrow, $\mathrm K$-theory and topological cyclic homology of henselian pairs
  • Bhatt-Clausen-Mathew, Remarks on $K(1)$-local $\mathrm K$-theory
  • Antieau-Mathew-Morrow-Nikolaus, On the Beilinson fiber square
  • Antieau-Krause-Nikolaus, On the K-theory of $\Z/p^n$

Stacky perspective / Prismatization

  • Bhatt, Prismatic $F$-gauges
  • Mondal, Reconstruction of the stacky approach to de Rham cohomology
  • Oberwolfach prismatization seminar
  • Drinfeld, Prismatization
  • Drinfeld, A 1-dimensional formal group over the prismatization of $\operatorname{Spf} \Z_p$
  • Bhatt-Lurie, The prismatization of $p$-adic formal schemes
  • Gurney, Prismatization over $\mathbf Z$
